Lost Moments

Every blink is a moment lost to the ages. We spontaneously blink every few seconds—15–20 per minute, blocking visual input for a period amounting to 10% of our waking hours. To overcome this human imperfection and to give back what is rightfully ours I have come up with a design of an apparatus that compensates us with a photograph for every blink we make.
